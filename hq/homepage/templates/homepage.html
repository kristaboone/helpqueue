Queued for Your Success

HQ allows students in a classroom to request the attention of the instructor by adding themself to a list. The instructor can answer the student's needs in the order the students asked the questions to prevent students from getting too lost.

Check it out!

Why use HQ?

Instructor Abilities

Set up your classroom, edit tables, add teaching assistants, and manage the queue.

Teaching Assistant Abilities

Assist the instructor in managing the queue.

Student Abilities

Add or remove yourself from the HQ if you need help in class.

Made with Love

Developed with benefitting education in mind.
